What is it?

LearningMe is a new way to study online. You create a personal account and with a simple questionare you will find courses tailored just for you!

  • Auditory Learning

    This type of learning style centers on hearing. People with this style of learning would rather listen to things being explained than read about them. Reciting information out loud and having music in the background may be a common study method.

  • Visual Learning

    Visual learners learn best by looking at graphics, watching a demonstration, or reading. For them it's easy to look at charts and graphs.

  • Kinesthetic Learners

    In this type of learning people process information best thorugh a "hands-on" experience. Doing an activity cna be the easiest way for them to learn.

Learning Styles

There are four main types of learning styles: auditory, visual and kinesthetic. Most people learn best through a combination of the three types of learning styles, but everybody is different.

How does it work?

The purpose of LearningMe is not just to get you a specialized way to study. But to guide you through other styles of learning so one day you can learn with those styles as well!
