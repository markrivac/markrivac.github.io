LearningMe

Your way of study.

What is it?

Students do not like to study. This can be for different types of learning, because they are well organized, they can not find a practical way to study or simply because they do not like it. But the main problem is that everyone has their own type of learning, and not knowing your kind of learning, studying becomes more difficult, tedious and boring, and you are not learning the right way. All this is worrying as it is reflected in the education of our country, and that is why we want to give a solution.

  • Auditory Learning

    This type of learning style centers on hearing. People with this style of learning would rather listen to things being explained than read about them. Reciting information out loud and having music in the background may be a common study method.

  • Visual Learning

    Vis¡ual learners learn best by looking at graphics, watching a demonstration, or reading. For them it's easy to look at charts and graphs.

  • Kinesthetic Learners

    In this type of learning people process information best thorugh a "hands-on" experience. Doing an activity cna be the easiest way for them to learn.

Learning Styles

There are three main types of learning styles: auditory, visual and kinesthetic. Most people learn best through a combination of the three types of learning styles, but everybody is different. That's one of the reason of LearningMe, we are going to help you to use all this kind of learning styles in one way expecting the best results from you in your education.

How does it work?

The purpose of LearningMe is not just to get you a specialized way to study. But to guide you through other styles of learning so one day you can learn with those styles as well!

LearningMe is a new way to study online. You create a personal account and with a simple questionare you will find courses tailored just for you!
